Auction for Loan Syndication D-App

This D-App shows the Auction section of our loan syndication smart contract built on the Ethereum blockchain.


How it works

Dutch Auction (lowest interest rate bidder wins):

  • Borrower deploys an auction contract to competing underwriters to get the lowest interest rate for its loan.
  • Bid amount (msg.value) is set as the full loan amount (amount is in wei)
  • Interest rate bid must be lower than existing bid (all rates are in basis points)
  • Contract is deployed in the Ropsten network


Existing Loans
